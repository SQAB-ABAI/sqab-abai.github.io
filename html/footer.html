Privacy

I'm keeping it short and sweet. I don't collect any data aside from error logging (i.e., program crashes or something). This is by design, because I hate ads and tracking people on the internet creeps me out. Even when logging errors, everyone's IP is anonymized through Google Analytics (check it, its in the footer). Also, this info is purged as early as google lets it, I think about 14 months. If you don't want any logging, click the "Do not allow cookies" or clear the cookie from this site. You're free to use the site either way.
