Annual Conference


The Society for the Quantitative Analyses of Behavior (SQAB) hosts a yearly conference coinciding with the Annual conference for the Association for Behavior Analysis International (ABAI). Topics span a range of human and non-human experimental research and both paper and poster sessions are offered. The main conference proceedings will take place at the Baton Convention and Exhibition Center (BCEC) in Room 205AB and the poster sessions in Room 205C.

Registration for SQAB 2022 is now open and the deadline for submissions is Monday April 11, 2022!.

Registration and additional information is available on the SQAB 2022 Registration page.
