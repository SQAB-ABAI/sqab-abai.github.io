Annual Conference


The Society for the Quantitative Analyses of Behavior (SQAB) hosts a yearly conference coinciding with the Annual conference for the Association for Behavior Analysis International (ABAI). Topics span a range of human and non-human experimental research and both paper and poster sessions are offered.

Registration for SQAB 2021 is now open! The conference will be available as both a livestream as well as viewable at a later time through the BehaviorLive service.

Registration and additional information is available on the SQAB 2021 Registration page.

SQAB 2021 will take place May 22/23 (Virtually)
