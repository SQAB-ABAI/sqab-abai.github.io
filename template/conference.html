Annual Conference


The Society for the Quantitative Analyses of Behavior (SQAB) hosts a yearly conference coinciding with the Annual conference for the Association for Behavior Analysis International (ABAI). Topics span a range of human and non-human experiemental research and both paper and poster sessions are offered.

Instructions for Poster Presenters

Poster sessions begin at 7:00PM and end at 9:30PM in the Vevey 4 Ballroom in the Swissôtel Chicago. Unfortunately posters cannot be hung in advance, though boards should be available by 6:30 pm, and all posters must be removed at the end of the session or will be disposed of by hotel staff.

Maximum poster size is 36"x36"

All posters have been assigned a number. The number can be found in your poster acceptance email, and posters are be listed by number in the conference program. Please hang your poster according to its number so that attendees can find you. To enable you to view other posters presented in your session,presenters are scheduled to be at their posters for either the first half of the session or the second half.

For even-numbered posters - please plan on standing by your poster from 7:30 pm – 8:15 pm

For odd-numbered posters - please plan on standing by your poster from 8:15 pm – 9:30 pm.
