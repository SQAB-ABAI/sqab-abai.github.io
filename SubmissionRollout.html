Submissions for Annual Conference (Pilot Testing)


Submissions for the 2019 Annual Conference (e.g., posters, papers) can be submitted only via file upload. Files must be submitted as a completed PDF form. Instructions regarding how to obtaining and submit the form are provided below.

Submission Instructions


First, download the Submission Form here.

Second, complete the form with all required information (i.e, poster, paper) and save the file with your last name followed by the year (e.g., "Lastname 2019.pdf").

Lastly, upload your submission form here and include your full name and your email contact.
